    • You have any First Aid service available in the park?
    First Aid kits are kept with every ride operator & at the office opp. Appu Columbus.

    Do you have any rest rooms in the park?
    Yes there are 2 rest rooms (toilets) in the park one opposite the canteen and other near Merry Cups.

    On which day is the Park closed?
    Park is open on 365days of the year.

    What are the timings of the park?
    On week days 12 noon to 8 p.m.
    On weekends and holidays 12 noon to 9.30 p.m.

    How many hours does it take to enjoy the whole park?
    If you take all the rides it takes 2 to 3 hours during peak season.

    Is there any arrangement for announcement system??
    Yes we have Public Announcement System at the office opposite Appu Columbus.

    How many thrill rides are there in your park?
    Roller Coaster, My Fair lady, Bhoot Bangala, Appu Colulmus are thrill at Appu Ghar.

    Is there space for parking?
    Yes there is ample parking space available.
